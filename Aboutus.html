Whats in store

SaveMart has a wide selection of clothing in store and with thousands of items arriving each day. Pants, shoes, shirts, socks and more, for all genders and ages. SaveMart will full your wardrobe at a low price.

About SaveMart

SaveMart is New Zealands favorite recycled clothing store. With outlets located across the country there's a bargain shoppers heaven not too far away from you. See Locations for your nearest store.

Overflowing wardrobe

Need to empty the wardrobe? SaveMart has a sustainable solution. Just go to your nearest blue clothing bin and deposit them. We will do the rest.
